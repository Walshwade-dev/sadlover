About MINGUNI

The Journey

Nzeki, known by his stage name Sad-lover, is a heartfelt Kamba vocalist, songwriter, and performer hailing from Machakos County, Kenya. Since 2018, he has captivated audiences with deeply emotional love songs, exploring themes of romance, heartbreak, and modern love struggles through poetic lyrics and powerful vocals. Beyond his musical endeavors, Daniel is also a gifted storyteller, novelist, and scriptwriter, weaving narratives that resonate with the profound essence of love and pain.

Musical Inspiration

Sad-lover draws inspiration from the rich Kamba musical heritage, blending traditional sounds with contemporary influences. His music is a fusion of soulful melodies and rhythmic beats, creating a unique sound that resonates with both local and international audiences. Through his songs, he aims to connect with listeners on an emotional level, sharing stories that reflect the complexities of love and relationships.
